A RISKY BUSINESS

One of the riskiest businesses is oil and gas (O&G) exploration. With only 25% of successful wells in the world, explorationists want to make sure they are targeting adequate formations that would avoid million-dollars losses. The risk climbs exponentially if it is taking place in a virgin basin, with unproven commercial potential. O&G projects are risky ventures due to their complex nature, potential environmental impact and high operational costs.

The goal of this visualization system is to give explorationists a first-hand overview of the source rock by analyzing their geochemical parameters.

Especial emphasis has been put into the source rocks lately due to their source-reservoir behaviour as the shale industry keeps booming. By analyzing the data embedded in the visualization, explorationists not only can get an overview of the geochemical behaviour of different formations but also their production potential which is highly valuable in the industry.
